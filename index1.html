Granby Colleges of Science and Technology – Naic, Cavite

The Granby Library serves as the academic backbone of Granby Colleges of Science and Technology, supporting students, faculty, and researchers across all levels of education–from elementary to college. As part of a private, non-sectarian institution recognized by CHED, TESDA, and DepEd, our library is committed to providing comprehensive learning resources that align with the institution's mission to make quality education accessible, especially to underprivileged communities.

Established alongside the college’s founding in 1999, the Granby Library has evolved from humble beginnings to a modern academic hub. We provide access to an extensive collection of books, journals, digital materials, and research tools that support various programs including Education, Information Technology, Criminology, Accountancy, Business Administration, and Tourism Management, as well as the K-12 Academic and TVL tracks.

With a strong emphasis on community service, environmental consciousness, and cultural responsibility, the Granby Library encourages students to grow not just academically but also as responsible citizens. We invite you to explore our catalog, digital archives, and student support services as you embark on your learning journey.
